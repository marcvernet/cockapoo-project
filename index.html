What is a Cockapoo?

The World's Most Adorable Designer Dog

A Cockapoo is one of the first "designer dogs" — a wonderful cross between a Cocker Spaniel and a Poodle. Bred for their hypoallergenic coats and irresistibly friendly personalities, cockapoos have captured hearts worldwide.

These charming companions come in four sizes — Teacup, Toy, Miniature, and Standard — each with their own unique personality while sharing the same loving spirit.
